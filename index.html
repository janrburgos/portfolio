Online Appointment System

A full stack application that lets an applicant apply for documents online. This application was inspired by MARINA government agency's MISMO online appointment system. This full stack web app solves the problem of an applicant spending a whole day's worth of time waiting in line on an office for processing documents and shortens it into one hour and also avoiding the hassle of bringing wrong or incomplete documents on appointment day. This app uses several npm packages like Moment.js for setting up appointment date, Multer for uploading document images into the server, and JSPDF for dynamically creating the document applied for by the applicant.

Digital Clock

A digital clock created using create-react-app. You can change the font style and font color of the clock by pressing each of the buttons below the clock as well as changing the clock format between 12 hour and 24 hour. The background color also becomes lighter when it becomes closer to noon and darker when it becomes closer to midnight.
